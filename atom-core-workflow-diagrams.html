Qualified?
Stage Gate: Qualify Decision
✗ Not Qualified
Move to Nurture (P4) or Disqualify (P5). Keep in Lead — do NOT create a Deal. Re-enter pipeline when criteria met.
✓ Qualified — Convert Lead
Convert Lead to Contact + Company + Deal in HubSpot. This is required for funnel analytics to track the full Lead→Close path.

Power BI Analytics Dependency — Why Lead → Deal Conversion is Required
Problem
Broken Funnel
Deals created without Lead conversion break the top-of-funnel metrics — no Lead source, no contact origin, incomplete pipeline attribution
Required Process
Lead → Deal
Every Deal must originate from a converted Lead. HubSpot enforces the association, enabling full funnel tracking from first touch to close
Power BI Benefit
Clean Reporting
Accurate stage conversion rates, lead-to-close velocity, source attribution, team performance metrics, and forecast accuracy across all 8 CORE stages
